  <img width="50" height="50" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a0bdd64-5bb6-46df-89f0-ecce3e7cfb25" /> **PROJECT OVERVIEW**


  This project analyzes the **Superstore dataset** to understand **sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and business trends**.  
The goal is to generate **actionable insights** to help improve decision-making.

<img width="50" height="50" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ae316e47-f34c-4333-a5c4-d22b08a3a8b6" /> **OBJECTIVES**


   Analyze **overall sales and profit performance**

   
   Identify **top and low-performing categories**

   
   Study **customer and regional behavior**

   
   Understand the **impact of discounts on profit**

   
   Detect **business risks and opportunities**

<img width="50" height="50" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8d8b3b7d-dd42-4c75-ac9f-7e26ea465626" />**KEY INSIGHTS**

  Technology category contributes the **highest sales**
  
  High discounts often lead to **negative profit**
  
  Certain sub-categories consistently generate **loss**
  
  West region shows **strong profitability**
  
  A small group of customers contribute to **major revenue**

 <img width="50" height="50" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/655b433a-3555-4bdb-a6ad-059684dac339" /> **BUSINESS RISKS & OPPORTUNITES**
 
  RISKS:
  
  Excessive discounting reduces profit margins
  
  Loss-making sub-categories impact overall profitability
  
  OPPORTUNITIES:
  
  Optimize discount strategy
  
  Focus on high-margin categories
  
  Retain high-value customers
